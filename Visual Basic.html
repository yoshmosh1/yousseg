Visual Basic Founders:-

Alan Cooper is known as the father of Visual Basic, a title given to him by Mitchell Waite, who wrote The Waite Group's Visual
Basic How-To, the first book written on the subject. Always a little different from other kids, Alan grew up with the notion of the
counterculture of the 1960s.
